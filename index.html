Web Tools

A small collection of free, browser-based utilities. No login required — everything runs locally in your browser and your data never leaves your device.

🍷 WSET Level 3 Wine Tasting Notes

Interactive tasting note form based on the WSET Level 3 Systematic Approach to Tasting Wine (SAT). Fill in appearance, nose, palate and conclusions, then export as JPG, CSV or plain-text summary. Great for wine students, sommeliers and enthusiasts.

📖 Rural Crypto Literature

A reading utility for crypto-related literature and resources.
